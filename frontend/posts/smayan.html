Smayan Srikanth

Smayan Srikanth is a passionate sports writer and analyst, dedicated to bringing fresh perspectives and in-depth analysis to the world of sports. With a keen eye for detail and a love for storytelling, Smayan aims to engage readers with insightful articles that explore the nuances of various sports.

Smayan's writing covers a wide range of topics, from player profiles and game analyses to the cultural impact of sports. With a background in journalism and a lifelong passion for athletics, Smayan strives to connect with readers through compelling narratives and thought-provoking commentary.

Whether it's the thrill of a last-minute goal, the drama of a championship match, or the inspiring stories of athletes overcoming challenges, Smayan's articles aim to capture the essence of sports and its ability to unite people across the globe.

Smayan is currently the lead writer and CEO of Sports Square. He is also the Los Angeles Dodgers beat writer for LWOSports.com. Smayan is open to writing for anyone.

For inquiries, collaborations, or to share your thoughts on sports, feel free to reach out via email at sportssquareauthor@gmail.com!
